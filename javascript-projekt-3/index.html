Strategy

Preperation

Before the start of Qi its important to chose which class you should focus. In total there are 3 classes, red, blue and producers. Reds and Blues are the attackes/deffenders get progress by attacking or defending a node, Producers, they need to gift coins and supplies, goods and army to get progress. Chose the type you prefer the most.

City

Building your city is really important if you want go get far in the map. You can use it for producing troops, goods and decorations. You can build a lot of different buildings, houses, production building, goods buildings, army building, decorations, cultural building and roads. Houses give coins, production building give supplies, goods building give gooods, army building give army, decorations give bonuses for fighting on map, cultural buildings give happiness and roads connect your city. Here is a step-by-step to help build your own city.

Map

Map is the place where you get progress, its like guild expedition, but the whole guild can contribute. There are different types of nodes: attaking (red), defending(blue) and gifting(yellow) and these can be seperated into more groups. Sword type is for attacking and defending and is the easiest difficulty, then there is the knight type same as the sword type, but more difficult, after there is the reinforcement type, which give bonuses to you, then there is the fortress type, which is as difficult as the knight type, but it gives bonuses to the boss type enemies, the most difficult type of them all.
